Our Mission

Our 501(c) (3) Organization is committed to providing necessities such as Socks, blankets, soap, shampoo, clothing, and other specialty services to the homeless community.

Our Founder, is dedicated to this organization with the belief that the homeless are not just “throw away people.”

With your support and contributions, we continue to provide our homeless communities with basic necessities that most of us take for granted.

Remember Even in the summertime the world can be a very cold place.
